How we started?

We are Codyle – the bewildering development company made for you. As university students we have always wanted a platform that can provide technology related events alongside relatable blogs of self-improvement and more. In addition, transforming what we love into services others can benefit from. That was when Codyle was born, it is a platform we developed on our own that satisfies the needs of university students, business starters, business owners and even technology enthusiasts.

Why choose us?

Because we’ll understand your brand, we’re easy to work with and we focus on results. Before we even start performing our services, we think about your tone of voice and value proposition, so that every move we make builds your brand. For us, it’s all about what adds value for you and your business.
